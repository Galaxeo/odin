Katsu Recipe

Description

Katsu is a Japanese dish of crispy fried cutlets coated with Panko bread crumbs.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown
