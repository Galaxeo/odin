Cornbread Recipe

Description

Skip the store-bought mix and make cornbread from scratch. This homemade cornbread recipe is perfectly moist, subtly sweet, and pairs perfectly with so many of your favorite comfort foods.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Whisk flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt together
  3. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean
